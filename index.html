What are all these squares?

  1. The green square is the head of the snake!
  2. The purple squares are the body of the snake!
  3. The blue square is the end of the tail of the snake!
  4. The red square is a juicy apple, every apple you collect, the snake will grow larger. As will your score!
  5. The brown squares are tree stumps that you will have to get around!

How it works

This application uses web workers to offload the processing.
This allows the browser UI to remain responsive even though CPU intensive operations may be taking place.
The results are drawn on the screen using a canvas element.
